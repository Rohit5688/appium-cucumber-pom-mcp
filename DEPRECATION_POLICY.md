# Deprecation Policy

AppForge follows a structured deprecation policy to ensure stability for users.

## Notice Period
- Any major breaking change or deprecation of a tool/handler will be announced at least 1 month prior to removal.
- Minor deprecations within components will be marked as deprecated for at least 1 minor version before deletion.

## Communication
- Deprecations will be clearly documented in the `CHANGELOG.md`.
- Tool descriptions affected will include a `[DEPRECATED]` tag.
