## 🏗️ 1. The Modernization Lifecycle

As your app scales, use the AppForge maintenance toolchain to harden your structural integrity.

<Steps>

1.  ### Dependency Audit (`upgrade_project`)
    Automatically identifies and upgrades outdated Appium drivers and WebdriverIO packages.
2.  ### Structural Correction (`repair_project`)
    Regenerates missing baseline files if they were accidentally deleted or corrupted during a merge.
3.  ### Quality Refactoring (`suggest_refactorings`)
    The engine identifies duplicate step definitions and unused Page Object methods, suggesting "Clean-Code" consolidations.
4.  ### Syncing the Brain (`analyze_codebase`)
    Regularly re-index your project to ensure the AI's "Structural Brain" is perfectly aligned with your latest architectural changes.

</Steps>

## 📈 3. Scaling Multi-Team Projects

In enterprise environments, AppForge enforces governance through structural consistency.

- **Standardized Hooks**: Use the generated global hooks to ensure every test session follows the same setup/teardown security protocols.
- **Shared Step Indexing**: By running `analyze_codebase`, teams can discover existing step definitions across multiple feature areas, preventing "Double-Coding."

## 🧠 4. Maintenance Knowledge Audit

<details>
<summary>**Q1: How often should I run `upgrade_project`?**</summary>
**Pro Hint**: We recommend running it monthly. Mobile drivers evolve rapidly, and staying current ensures compatibility with the latest Android and iOS versions.
</details>

<details>
<summary>**Q2: What happens if `repair_project` overwrites my custom code?**</summary>
**Answer**: It won't. `repair_project` only regenerates missing *baseline* files (configs, hooks). It will never overwrite your custom Page Objects or Feature files.
</details>
